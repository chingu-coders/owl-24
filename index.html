Theme - Stylesheet Generator for Reddit

This is a stylesheet generator for styling your subreddit. To start, click the theme that you want. V0.17

 

Now, paste the theme into your subreddit at this link: https://www.reddit.com/r/YOURSUBREDDIT/about/stylesheet.

Lastly, download the icons below, and upload them into your subreddit. For the theme to work, you need to name the images exactly as they are named in the list. For a more detailed tutorial, click here to see a video on uploading your icons.
